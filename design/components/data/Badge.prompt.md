**Badge** — small status pill for record states. Soft tints by default; `solid` for high emphasis; `dot` for a status indicator.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Pagado</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" dot>Pendiente</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" dot>Vencido</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral">Retirado</Badge>
```

Tones: neutral·brand·success·warning·danger·info·accent. Modifiers: `solid`, `pill`, `size="sm"`, `dot`.
